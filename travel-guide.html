Getting to Plovdiv

By Air

Plovdiv Airport (PDV) - The city's international airport with connections to major European cities.

  • Direct flights from London, Vienna, and other cities
  • Airport to city center: 20-30 minutes by taxi

Sofia Airport (SOF) - Bulgaria's main international airport, 150 km from Plovdiv.

  • More international connections available
  • Train or bus to Plovdiv: 2-2.5 hours
  • Private transfer available (contact us for arrangements)

By Train

Plovdiv has excellent train connections:

  • From Sofia: 2-2.5 hours (regular services)
  • From Istanbul: 5-6 hours (direct train available)
  • Plovdiv Central Station is well-connected to the city center

By Car

If you prefer driving:

  • From Sofia: 150 km via the A1/E79 motorway (1.5-2 hours)
  • From Istanbul: 550 km via the A1/E80 (5-6 hours)
  • Ample parking available at the venue

Exploring Plovdiv

While you're here, discover one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities:

  • Ancient Roman Stadium - 2nd century AD, right in the city center
  • Roman Theatre - Stunning 1st century amphitheatre, still in use
  • Old Town - Ottoman-era architecture and museums
  • Kapana Creative District - Trendy area with cafes, galleries, and street art
  • Rowing Canal - Beautiful park for morning walks
